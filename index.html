Welcome to the Inaugural ATD Oman Conference & EXPO

Innovate, Inspire, Impact: Redefining the Future of Work

The ATD Oman Conference & EXPO 2026 will bring together leading minds in HR, talent, and learning and development to explore pioneering approaches reshaping the workplace of tomorrow. The 2-day conference will focus on driving innovation, empowering leadership, and creating meaningful impact through strategic talent management.

Attendees will explore cutting-edge trends and best practices in digital transformation, agile work environments, and workforce development. The conference will also highlight the importance of cultivating a forward-thinking organizational culture, empowering employees, and leveraging technology to drive sustainable growth.

To maximize your learning experience, we’re also offering four globally recognized ATD certificate programs, giving you the opportunity to enhance your expertise and stay ahead in the industry. Join us and take the next step in transforming talent and driving innovation in your organization!
